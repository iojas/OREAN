OREAN, Omics REsearch ANalytics, is an interactive web-based multi-omics data analysis, exploration, visualization and collaboration system. OREAN supports various interactive and dynamic data-exploratory visualizations and metadata-driven case-control comparative statistical analyses such as alpha/beta diversity, PCA, lefse, heatmap, hierarchical clustering etc. OREAN provides an intuitive way to share and collaborate on datasets by multi-skillset domain experts and data scientists.

Features:
  • Cohort Discovery and Exploration
  • Visualization
  • Statistical Analysis
  • Data Collaboration
Technology:
  • Python
  • Django
  • Celery
  • MySQL
  • R
  • HighCharts
  • D3.js
